Structured Hints for Sample-Efficient Lean Theorem Proving

Testing whether neural theorem provers benefit from lightweight structural guidance during inference. A simple intervention using 15 common tactic skeletons achieves 21.7% pass@16 compared to 15.2% for standard sampling (43% relative improvement) on miniF2F.

Collision Thresholds on Lattices

This paper studies the threshold behavior of collisions in a system of independent random walks on the integer lattice, using Poissonization and generating function asymptotics. We derive a sharp threshold for the expected number of pairwise collisions and establish asymptotic expressions via the Laplace method.
